School Policies





SCHOOL TEN COMMANDMENT

This rules are to be followed and failure to do so is prohibited.


First commandment = Early and Punctual: All student/pupils are to be right on time before 07:30.

Second commandment = Students/pupils are to be compeletly dressed before coming to school.

Third commandment = Respect Thy Self and Others: Treat others with kindness, empathy and respect.

Forth commandment = Learn with Integrity: Always do your own work and give cerdit where it's due.

Fifth commandment = Be prepard: Come to class ready to learn with all necessary materials.

Sixth commandment = Listen and Follow Direction: pay attention to teachers and follow instructions carefully.

Seventh commandment = Be kind and inclusive: Welcome and include others, regardless of differences.

Eighth commandment = Strive for Excellence: Give your best effort in all aspects of school life.

Ninth commandment = Stay safe and Healthy: Prioritize your physical and mental well-being

Tenth commandment = Embody school values: uphold the values of our school community including respect, responsibility and resilience.
